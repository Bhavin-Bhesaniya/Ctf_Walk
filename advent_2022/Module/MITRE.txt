Basic Terminology :-
 - Advanced Persistent Threat(APT) Can be Considered Threat group or nation-state group that engages in long-term attacks against organizations and/or countries
 
 Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures(TTP) :-
  Tactics   - Goal or objective
  Technique - How adversary achieves the goal or objective
  Procedure - How Technique executed



ATT&CK Framework :-
 - Globally-accessible knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques based on real-world observation
 - Document Common TTP used by APT against enterprise windows attacks
 - Intiall project FMX(Fort Meade Experiment) Where Professional Collect data to construct what we today known as ATTACK Framework

 Phishing :-
  - Electronically delivered social engineering that contain malicious attachment or links to execute malicious code on vicitim systems to gather credentials
  Spearphishing - Individual Company or industry will be targeted 



Cyber Analytics Repository(CAR):-
 - knowledge base of analytics developed by MITRE
 - Define data model that is leveraged in its pseudocode representation but also includes implementations directly targeted at specific tools
 Event Query Language(EQL) :- Utilized to query, parse, and organize Sysmon event data
